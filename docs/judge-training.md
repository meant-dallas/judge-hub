# Judge Training Guide

**MEANT JudgeHub** — Step-by-step guide for judges

---

## Overview

As a judge, your role is to score participants during a live event session. The coordinator controls the flow — they decide which participant is currently presenting. Your job is to submit scores for each participant as they present.

You will only see the participant the coordinator has set as **Now Presenting**. Scores are locked once you submit them.

---

## Step 1 — Sign in and select your event

Go to the JudgeHub URL and sign in with your Google account. You will land on the **My Events** page, which lists every event you have been assigned to judge.

![My Events page showing active and completed events](screenshots/01-my-events.png)

- Events marked **ACTIVE** are in progress — these are the ones you will be scoring.
- Events marked **COMPLETED** are finished. You can view them but cannot change scores.
- The progress counter (e.g. **0/2 scored**) shows how many participants you have submitted scores for out of your total assignment.

Click the event name to open it.

---

## Step 2 — Wait for the live session to begin

After opening an event, you will see the event detail page with a list of the participants assigned to you.

![Event detail showing NOW PRESENTING card and participant list](screenshots/02-event-detail-presenting.png)

Key areas on this page:

- **NOW PRESENTING** card — highlights the participant the coordinator has set as currently active. This is who you should score right now.
- **Score Now** button — appears only for the currently presenting participant. Click it to open the scoring form.
- **Scoring progress** — shows how many of your assigned participants you have scored so far (e.g. 0/2).
- **Participant list** — shows all participants assigned to you. The one currently presenting has a green dot (●) next to their name.

> If no participant has been set as presenting yet, the Score Now button will not appear. Wait for the coordinator to start the session.

---

## Step 3 — Fill in the scoring form

Click **Score Now** to open the scoring form for the presenting participant.

![Empty scoring form with four criteria](screenshots/03-scoring-form-empty.png)

The form shows each judging criterion with its maximum point value. Typical criteria:

| Criterion | Max Points |
|-----------|-----------|
| Introduction | 15 |
| Knowledge & Depth | 35 |
| Presentation & Delivery | 35 |
| Conclusion | 15 |
| **Total** | **100** |

Enter a score for each criterion. Scores must be between 0 and the maximum shown.

---

## Step 4 — Review your scores before submitting

As you fill in scores, the form updates in real time to show:

![Scoring form with scores entered and running total](screenshots/04-scoring-form-filled.png)

- **Blue progress bars** — visually show how many points you have awarded out of the maximum for each criterion.
- **Points remaining indicator** — shows how many more points are available for each criterion.
- **Running total** — the total score so far (e.g. 55/100).

Take a moment to review your scores. **Once you submit, scores are final and cannot be changed.**

---

## Step 5 — Submit your scores

When you are satisfied with your scores, click **Submit Scores**. You will see a confirmation screen:

![Scores submitted confirmation with green checkmark](screenshots/05-scores-submitted.png)

- The green checkmark confirms your scores were recorded.
- The message **"Waiting for coordinator to advance…"** means the coordinator has not yet moved to the next participant.
- Click **← Back to event overview** to return to the event page and wait for the next participant.

---

## Step 6 — Score the next participant

When the coordinator advances to the next participant, the event overview updates automatically on your next page load.

![Event overview showing next participant presenting, first participant submitted](screenshots/06-next-participant.png)

- The new **NOW PRESENTING** card shows the next participant.
- The previous participant now shows a **SUBMITTED** badge in your list — confirming your scores are locked in.
- The scoring progress updates (e.g. 1/2).

Repeat Steps 3–5 for each participant until all are scored.

---

## Summary

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Sign in → select your active event |
| 2 | Wait for NOW PRESENTING to appear |
| 3 | Click **Score Now** → fill in scores |
| 4 | Review running total and progress bars |
| 5 | Click **Submit Scores** → confirm green checkmark |
| 6 | Return to event overview → repeat for next participant |

---

## Tips

- **You can only score the currently presenting participant.** The Score Now button does not appear for participants who are not actively presenting.
- **Scores are final once submitted.** If you make an error, contact the event coordinator immediately — they can reach an admin to make corrections before the event is marked Complete.
- **Refresh the page** if you are waiting for the coordinator to advance and nothing seems to be changing. The page does not update automatically.
- **Your scores are independent.** Other judges cannot see your scores and you cannot see theirs. This ensures unbiased evaluation.

---

## Need help?

Contact your event coordinator if:
- You cannot see the event in your list
- The Score Now button is not appearing
- You submitted incorrect scores and need a correction
